List 13

Fiscal adj.

Meaning

财政的

Example

Asian countries have embraced this wisdom, and have built sound long-term fiscal policies as a result.

Fitting adj.

Meaning

适合的

Example

The dinner was a fitting end to Carter’s 25 years with the company.

Fixture n.

Meaning

固定装置

Example

The highway is an important fixture in this region.

Flank v.

Meaning

Example

Meaning

Example

Flare n.

Meaning

Example

Tempers flared during the debates.

Flatter v.

Meaning

Example

Fling v.

Meaning

People cheered and flung their hats into the air.

Flip v.

Meaning

Example

Meaning

Example

Meaning

Flush v.

Meaning

Example

Flutter v.

Meaning

Meaning

Example

Foil v.

Meaning

Example

Meaning

Example

Forfeit v.

Meaning

Example

If they moved away, they would forfeit all rights to their land.

Forge v.

Meaning

Example

Meaning

Example

Formulate v.

Meaning

Example

Fort n.

Meaning

Example

Meaning

加强

Example

Fortnight n.

Meaning

Example

In a fortnight‘s time I will be home.

Forum n.

Meaning

论坛

Example

Foul v.

Meaning

Example

We need to foul one of their guys in order to stop the clock.

Fracture v.

Meaning

折断

Example

Fragile adj.

Meaning

易碎的

Example

Fragrant adj.

Meaning

芬芳的

Example

The air in the garden was fragrant.

Meaning

Example

Frantic adj.

Meaning

慌乱不安的

Example

They made a frantic search for the lost child.

Fraud n.

Meaning

Example

Friction n.

Meaning

不和

Example

Fringe adj.

Meaning

附加的

Example

The environment is no longer a fringe issue.

Frown v.

Meaning

皱眉

Example

the manager frowned at being interrupted.

Furious adj.

Meaning

暴怒的

Example

The professor will be furious with us if we’re late again.

Furnish v.

Meaning

配备

Example

Bedrooms are simply furnished and have telephones.

Fury n.

Meaning

暴怒

Example

At last the fury of the storm lessened.

Fuse n.

Meaning

导火索

Example

Be sure all safety measures have been taken before lighting the fuse.

Fusion n.

Meaning

联合

Example

Singaporean culture is a successful fusion of Eastern and Western features.

Futile adj.

Meaning

徒劳的

Example

John’s efforts to return to school proved futile.

Garment n.

Meaning

衣服

Example

The profit of the garment industry in this area has grown by 20% in the past five years.

Gasp v.

Meaning

喘气

Example

They all
gasped and clutched each other.

Gauge v.

Meaning

估计

Example

I gauged that it was not a good moment to speak to the professor.

Genre n.

Meaning

体裁

Example

These writers can genuinely be said to have created a genre.

Gigantic adj.

Meaning

Example

This gigantic wave came in from the sea.

Giggle v.

Meaning

Example

Stop giggling, children; this is a serious matter.

Hawk v.

Example

China and on foreign investment.

Example

As autumn approaches, the beekeepers pack up their hives and go south.

Example

Tim hoisted the boy onto his shoulders.

We were delighted by the wonderful hospitality of the local people.

Example
