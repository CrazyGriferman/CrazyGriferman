List 16

Inflict v.

Meaning

使······遭受

Example

Who would inflict such an ordeal on a child?

Infringe v.

Meaning

侵犯

Example

A backup copy of a computer program does not infringe copyright.

Ingenious adj.

Meaning

巧妙的

Example

Many fish have ingenious ways of protecting their eggs from predators.

Ingenuity n.

Meaning

聪明才智

Example

The world has benefited from their ingenuity.

Inherit v.

Meaning

继承

Example

Jim has been living in the lap of luxury since he inherited his father’s money.

Inhibit v.

Meaning

抑制

Example

Childhood viral infections will inhibit the development of brain cells.

Initiate v.

Meaning

开始

Example

They have decided to initiate legal proceedings against the newspaper.

Inlet n.

Meaning

Example

There’s a thousand little inlets and backwaters all through here.

Meaning

（监狱或精神病院）同住者

Innumerable adj.

Meaning

Example

That trail has vanished, leaving innumerable unanswered questions.

Insane adj.

Meaning

Example

I know all about your insane plan.

Meaning

Example

Instrumental adj.

Meaning

Example

His information was instrumental in catching the criminal.

Meaning

Example

Meaning

Meaning

Example

Intelligible adj.

Meaning

Example

Interim adj.

Meaning

Intermittent adj.

Meaning

Example

Interrogate v.

Meaning

Example

Two suspects are now being interrogated in connection with the killing.

Intersection n.

Meaning

Example

Intervene v.

Meaning

Example

I shall call on you tomorrow if nothing intervenes.

Intimate adj.

Meaning

Example

Wendy told her mother about their intimate relationship.

Intimidate v.

Meaning

Example

Intricate adj.

Meaning

Example

Intrigue v.

Meaning

Example

They are intriguing against my friend.

Intrinsic adj.

Meaning

Example

Invalid adj.

Meaning

Meaning

Example

Meaning

Example

Invert v.

Meaning

Example

Irrespective adj.

Meaning

Example

Meaning

Example

Irritate v.

Meaning

Example

Meaning

象牙

Example
