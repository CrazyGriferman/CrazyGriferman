Chinese

English

掺假的酒

Chinese

Chinese

English

Chinese

促进经济发展

English

Chinese

提出一个新理论

English

the advent of a green economy n.

Chinese

绿色经济的出现

English

Chinese

总统的政敌

English

Chinese

与逆境作斗争

English

Chinese

商店的窗户上贴的招聘广告

Advocate free market v.

Chinese

主张自由市场经济

English

If you advocate a particular action or plan, you recommend it publicly.

Aerate your bedroom v.

Chinese

让卧室通一下风

Chinese

纽约市的航拍照片

Chinese

设计流线型的车型

Aesthetic attractions of his works adj.

Chinese

他作品的美

Chinese

of behaving, you pretend that it is genuine, or natural for you.

His affected manner adj.

Chinese

做作的举止

English

If you describe someone’s behavior as affected, you disapprove of the fact that they behave in an unnatural way that is intended to impress other people.

An affectionate couple adj.

Chinese

恩爱的夫妻

English

If you are affectionate, you show your love or fondness for another person in the way that you behave towards them.

A high school affiliated to a college v.

Chinese

高校的附属中学

English

An accident affirmed to be true v.

Chinese

经过确认的事故

English

If you affirm that something is true or that something exists, you state firmly and publicly that it is true or exists.

Her affirmative answer adj.

Chinese

肯定的答复

English

An affirmative word or gesture indicates that you agree with what someone has said or that the answer to a question is “yes”.

People afflicted by a natural disaster v.

Chinese

English

If you a re afflicted by pain, illness, or disaster, it affects you badly and makes you suffer.

Chinese

Chinese

Chinese

Chinese

Chinese
