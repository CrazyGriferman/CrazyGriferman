四级疑难词汇 list 6~10

发表于 2019-09-20 | 分类于 English-Learning | 评论数： | 阅读次数：

List 6

Cellar n.

Meaning

地窖

Example

He’s got an impressively large store of wine in his cellar.

Celsius n.

Meaning

摄氏度

Example

The thermometer recorded a temperature of 30 degrees Celsius.

Cereal n.

Meaning

谷物

Example

He tipped his breakfast cereal into a bowl and went to get the milk.

Chamber n.

Meaning

室

Example

When they wake up they found themselves in a vast underground chamber.

Champagne n.

Meaning

香槟酒

Example

The champagne tastes like juice.

Chaos n.

Meaning

混乱

Example

My room was in chaos caused by the children.

Charter v.

Meaning

租（飞机、汽车等）

Example

They chartered a car for traveling here.

Cherish v.

Meaning

珍视

Example

Above all, the interstate system provides individuals with what they cherish most: personal freedom of mobility.

Chin n.

Meaning

下巴

Example

To keep the helmet in position, fasten the strap beneath the chin.

Choke v.

Meaning

哽咽

Example

The girl found it hard to choke back her tears when she heard the bad news.

Cholesterol n.

Meaning

胆固醇

Example

Avoid fatty foods that are high in cholesterol.

Chop v.

Meaning

砍

Example

A couple of old trees have been chopped down for road construction.

Chore n.

Meaning

令人讨厌的工作

Example

Jenny’s husband thought shopping was a chore.

Chorus n.

Meaning

合唱队

Example

The newcomers added their voices to the chorus expressing delight at the result.

Christ n.

Meaning

基督

Circuit n.

Meaning

电路

Example

Is is dangerous to make the circuit overloaded.

Circus n.

Meaning

马戏

Example

There are posters all over the town advertising the circus.

Cite v.

Meaning

引用

Example

One such game, called of World of Warcraft, is cited on many sites by posters complaining of a “gaming addiction”.

List 7

Clause n.

Meaning

条款

Example

There is a clause in the contract requiring a fee to be paid if the holiday is cancelled.

Claw v.

Meaning

抓

Example

He was seriously injured when one of the lions clawed his back.

Clear-cut adj.

Meaning

明确的

Example

She has clear-cut evidence that the company cheated her.

Client n.

Meaning

委托人

Example

A final advertising medium is direct mail, which uses mailings to consumers to communicate a client‘s message.

Climax n.

Meaning

高潮

Example

The book’s powerful climax is the murder of Nancy by her lover Bill.

Cling v.

Meaning

坚持

Example

He clung to the belief that his family were alive after the earthquake.

Clip v.

Meaning

(用夹子、回形针) 夹住

Example

He collected up the papers and clipped them together.

Clumsy adj.

Meaning

笨拙的

Example

John is clumsy and can’t untie the knot.

Coarse adj.

Meaning

粗糙的

Example

She hates her coarse skin very much.

Coherent adj.

Meaning

一致的

Example

Those shareholders seemed to have no coherent plan for preventing the company from going bankrupt.

Cohesion n.

Meaning

凝聚力

Example

The article comments on the lack of cohesion and commitment within the administration.

Coil v.

Meaning

卷

Example

The machine was coiled by wool.

Coinage n.

Meaning

金属货币

Example

It took four years for Britain just to decimalise its own coinage.

Collaborate v.

Meaning

合作

Example

The boss would ask me to collaborate with him in this work.

Collision n.

Meaning

碰撞

Example

We still don’t know whether a collision might occur.

Colon n.

Meaning

冒号

Combat v.

Meaning

与······斗争

Example

Our brave soldiers were ready to combat the enemy.

Commence v.

Meaning

开始

Example

The term of this contract shall commence on June 21st.

Commission v.

Meaning

委任

Example

The best solution to this problem was to commission a famous architect.

Commodity n.

Meaning

商品

Example

The prices of the commodities are quite stable this year.

Commonplace adj.

Meaning

普通的

Example

It is now commonplace for people to use the Internet at home.

Commune n.

Meaning

公社

Example

She left her husband to join a women’s commune.

Commute v.

Meaning

经常乘车往返于两地

Example

He worked the land and commuted to the city to run his business.

Compact adj.

Meaning

紧凑的

Example

What a compact office! How did you fit so much into so little space?

Compass n.

Meaning

范围

Example

This problem is beyond the compass of my mind.

Compatible adj.

Meaning

兼容的

Example

This software cannot be compatible with our computer system.

Compel v.

Meaning

强迫

Example

Recent reports found that lodging with a student of a different race may decrease prejudice and compel students to engage in more ethnically diverse friendships

Compensate v.

Meaning

补偿

Example

The Labor Law asks companies to compensate workers for injuries suffered at their work.

Comply v.

Meaning

遵从

Example

Students should comply with the rules of school.

Compress v.

Meaning

压缩

Example

Rehearsal was compressed into two evenings because of the bad weather.

Comprise v.

Meaning

包含

Example

At that time, blacks comprised about 12% of the American population.

Compromise v.

Meaning

妥协

Example

Their conclusion was different, so they compromised.

Concede v.

Meaning

(不情愿地) 承认

Example

She had to concede that she had lied at the court.

List 8

Condemn v.

Meaning

谴责

Example

Henry last week condemned the attacks.

Condense v.

Meaning

压缩

Example

A month-long training course had been condensed into two weeks.

Confidential adj.

Meaning

秘密的

Example

A psychoanalyst who gives away confidential information about customers is not behaving professionally.

Conform v.

Meaning

服从（规则、法律）

Example

The products conformed to international safety standards.

Confucian n.

Meaning

儒家

Conjunction n.

Meaning

连接

Example

Seat belts are more effective when used in conjunction with air bags.

Conscientious adj.

Meaning

一丝不苟的

Example

This statue is the most conscientious piece of work.

Consensus n.

Meaning

（意见等）一致

Example

There’s still no consensus on how much time online constitutes too much or whether addiction is possible.

Consent n.

Meaning

同意

Example

They win the consent of their classmates.

Contaminate v.

Meaning

污染

Example

Most of the coast has been contaminated by nuclear waste.

Contempt n.

Meaning

轻视

Example

The report shows deep contempt for women’s judgment.

Continuity n.

Meaning

连续（性）

Example

The school was like a second family, providing continuity and stability.

Controversial adj.

Meaning

引起争议的

Example

Any research that suggests that abilities to perform certain behaviors are based in biology is controversial.

Controversy n.

Meaning

争论

Example

The election ended in controversy, with allegations of widespread vote-rigging.

Convention n.

Meaning

习俗

Example

By convention, a club member should resign in such a situation.

Conventional adj.

Meaning

常规的

Example

Internet connections through conventional phone lines are fairly slow.

Convict vt.

Meaning

证明······有罪

Example

So far as I knew, there wasn’t enough evidence to convict her.

Meaning

警察

Copper n.

Meaning

铜

Example

One of the properties of copper is that it conducts heat and electricity very well.

Copyright n.

Meaning

版权

Example

The news report is protected by copyright.

Cord n.

Meaning

细绳

Example

The door had been tied shut with a length of nylon cord.

Corporate adj.

Meaning

公司的

Example

Some companies are introducing ethics into their corporate culture in a big way.

Corps n.

Meaning

部队

Example

Donna Fredrick’s served with the Peace Corps for two years in Brazil.

Corrupt v.

Meaning

使腐败

Example

Greed corrupted the mayor.

Costly adj.

Meaning

昂贵的

Example

In the Great Depression many unhappy couples chose to stick together because living separately would be too costly.

Costume n.

Meaning

服装

Example

I didn’t like the drama much but the costumes were amazing.

Council n.

Meaning

理事会

Example

the UN Security Council

Counsel v.

Meaning

提议

Example

She has spent about five years counselling rape victims.

Counterpart n.

Meaning

与对方地位相当的人

Example

American officials are discussing this matter with their French counterparts.

County n.

Meaning

郡县

Example

A county usually consists of several towns and the rural areas which surround them.

Coupon n.

Meaning

优惠券

Example

The store owner gave me a couple of money-off coupons.

Courtyard n.

Meaning

庭院

Coverage n.

Meaning

新闻报道

Example

The murder has received extensive media coverage.

Crab n.

Meaning

蟹

Example

All the shops on the seafront had crab for sale.

Cracker n.

Meaning

爆竹

Crane n.

Meaning

起重机

Example

The little prefabricated hut was lifted away by a huge crane.

Creep v.

Meaning

爬行

Example

We took off our shoes and crept cautiously on the roof

Criterion n.

Meaning

标准

Example

What is the criterion for hiring new employees.

Crocodile n.

Meaning

鳄鱼

Crossing n.

Meaning

人行横道

Example

You must give way to any pedestrians on the crossing.

Crude adj.

Meaning

简陋的

Example

Birthplace data are only the crudest indicator of actual migration paths.

Cruise v.

Meaning

航游

Example

I’d love to go on a round-the-world cruise.

Crust n.

Meaning

外壳

Example

As the water evaporates, a crust of salt is left on the surface of the soil.

Curb v.

Meaning

约束

Example

If we curb production, we can see some increase in price.

Curl v.

Meaning

卷曲

Example

The singer’s hair curls naturally, which is in fashion.

Curse v.

Meaning

诅咒

Example

The family had been cursed by a witch for nearly a century.

Cyberspace n.

Meaning

网络空间

Example

In cyberspace, reward comes only to those who are willing to take chance.

Damn v.

Meaning

严厉批评

Example

The movie was damned by the critics for its violence and the tendency to seduce audiences into committing crimes.

List 10

Dash v.

Meaning

猛冲

Example

Maria came dashing down the stairs.

Dean n.

Meaning

系主任

Example

The dean of the Foreign Languages Department will have a lecture on translation this afternoon.

Declaration n.

Meaning

宣布

Example

The series of declarations and conventions adopted by the United Nations have won the support and respect of many countries.

Deem v.

Meaning

认为

Example

I deem it wise to put on my coat.

Defect n.

Meaning

缺点

Example

There are so many defects in our education system.

Deficient adj.

Meaning

缺乏的

Example

Bill is a hardworking worker, but he is deficient in courage.

Deficit n.

Meaning

赤字

Example

The country is running a balance-of-trade deficit of $250 million.

Defy v.

Meaning

（公然）违抗

Example

The man openly defied the law.

Dent v.

Meaning

挫伤（信心、名誉等）

Example

His confidence was badly dented when he didn’t get into the football team.

Deputy n.

Meaning

副手

Example

I’m acting as deputy while the boss is away.

Descend v.

Meaning

下降

Example

The path descended steeply into the valley.

Despatch v.

Meaning

派遣

Example

The Italian government was preparing to dispatch 4,000 soldiers to search the island.

Deteriorate v.

Meaning

恶化

Example

The environment of the Global Village is deteriorating, as a result of the industrial pollution and people’s lack of awareness.

Devil n.

Meaning

魔鬼

Dew n.

Meaning

露水

Example

The dew gathered on the leaves.

Diabetes n.

Meaning

糖尿病

Diameter n.

Meaning

直径

Example

The diameter measures twice the radius.

Dictate v.

Meaning

命令

Example

The UN will dictate the terms of troop withdrawal from the region.
