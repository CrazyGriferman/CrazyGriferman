四级疑难词汇 list 11~15

发表于 2019-09-21 | 更新于 2019-10-26 | 分类于 English-Learning | 评论数： | 阅读次数：

List 11

Dilemma n.

Meaning

窘境

Example

The boss was in a dilemma.

Dimension n.

Meaning

规模

Example

The strikes had now taken on an important political dimension.

Discard v.

Meaning

丢弃

Example

Once we start to see people as individuals, and discard the stereotypes, we can move positively toward inclusiveness for everyone.

Discharge v.

Meaning

释放

Example

The factory was fined for discharging chemicals into the river.

Disguise v.

Meaning

伪装

Example

He didn’t disguise his bitterness about what had happened.

Disgust n.

Meaning

厌恶

Example

The idea of eating meat fills me with disgust.

Disintegrate v.

Meaning

解体

Example

When governments lose their control on power, law and order begin to disintegrate.

Dispute v.

Meaning

争论

Example

Few would dispute that travel broadens the mind.

Disrupt v.

Meaning

使中断

Example

A heavy fall of snow had disrupted the city’s transport system.

Distress n.

Meaning

忧虑

Example

Two in distress makes sorrow less.

Ditch n.

Meaning

沟

Dizzy adj.

Meaning

头晕目眩的

Example

The supervisor of the laboratory is beginning to get headaches and dizzy spells because she says it’s dangerous to breathe some of the chemical smoke there.

Dock n.

Meaning

码头

Example

She headed for the docks, thinking that Ricardo might be hiding in one of the boats.

Doom n.

Meaning

厄运

Example

He predicted doom for any country that did not act immediately.

Dorm n.

Meaning

宿舍

Dose n.

Meaning

剂量

Example

If you are in pain, increase the dose of painkillers.

Dove n.

Meaning

鸽子

List 12

Dropout n.

Meaning

中途退学

Example

While dropout rates for all freshmen at American universities is around 20 percent, the rate for online students is percent.

Dual adj.

Meaning

双重的

Example

This room has a dual purpose, serving as both a study and a dining room.

Dumb adj.

Meaning

哑的

Example

He’s been deaf and dumb since birth.

Durable adj.

Meaning

持久的

Example

Durable friendships can be very difficult to maintain.

Duration n.

Meaning

持续

Example

The church was used as a temporary hospital for the duration of the war.

Eagle n.

Meaning

鹰

Example

Above the mountain, eagles circled in soundless flight.

Echo v.

Meaning

发出回声

Example

Her question echoed around the room.

Elastic adj.

Meaning

灵活的

Example

For there are too many uncertainties, we made an elastic plan.

Elbow n.

Meaning

肘

Example

She sat with her elbows on the table.

Elevate v.

Meaning

提高

Example

We need to work together to elevate the position of women in society.

Elite n.

Meaning

社会精英

Example

A powerful and corrupt elite had bled this country.

Embassy n.

Meaning

大使馆

Example

The British embassy declined to comment.

Emit v.

Meaning

散发

Example

Suddenly, the metal container began to emit a strange sound.

Endanger v.

Meaning

危及

Example

Smoke would endanger your health.

Enquire v.

Meaning

询问

Example

I called the railway station to enquire about train schedules.

List 13

Entrepreneur n.

Meaning

企业家

Example

Research reveals that the youngest child of the family in Scotland is more likely to be an entrepreneur.

Episode n.

Meaning

插曲

Example

It was just an episode in Mike’s life that he is not proud of.

Equation n.

Meaning

方程（式）

Example

In the equation 2x + 1 = 7, what is x?

Erroneous adj.

Meaning

错误的

Example

Some people have the erroneous notion that one can contract AIDS by giving blood.

Eternal adj.

Meaning

永恒的

Example

She has earned our eternal gratitude.

Ethic n.

Meaning

道德准则

Example

The ethics of journalism are much debated.

Ethnic adj.

Meaning

种族的

Example

The country’s population consists of three main ethnic groups.

Excel v.

Meaning

突出

Example

People coming here excel in their respective fields.

Excess n.

Meaning

过量

Example

An excess of houseplants in a small flat can be oppressive.

Exclaim v.

Meaning

呼喊

Example

The boy exclaimed so angrily that everyone looked at him.

Exert v.

Meaning

尽（力）

Example

You must exert yourself to pass your exam.

Exile n.

Meaning

流放

Example

The famous officer was sent into exile.

Explicit adj.

Meaning

详述的

Example

The relatives hope the doctor could give them an explicit answer.

Expressway n.

Meaning

高速公路

Exterior adj.

Meaning

外部的

Example

Exterior to the main house there is a small building that could be used as an office or studio.

List 14

Fabric n.

Meaning

织物

Example

Whatever your color scheme, there’s a fabric to match.

Faculty n.

Meaning

全体教师

Example

In China many newly hired faculty members at the top research universities received their graduate education abroad.

Famine n.

Meaning

饥荒

Example

Another crop failure could result in widespread famine.

Fare v.

Meaning

进展

Example

How did you fare in the examination?

Fatigue n.

Meaning

疲劳

Example

A child’s sleep problems cause parents fatigue and unnecessary guilt.

Faulty adj.

Meaning

有故障的

Example

We do not have enough money to repair the faulty equipment.

Federation n.

Meaning

联合会

Example

One of the states has seceded from the federation.

Fibre n.

Meaning

纤维

Example

Fruit and vegetables are high in fibre content.

Filter v.

Meaning

透（过）

Example

Sunlight filtered through the drawn shades.

Fireplace n.

Meaning

壁炉

Fleet n.

Meaning

舰队

Example

The damage inflicted upon the British fleet was devastating

Fluctuate v.

Meaning

波动

Example

During the economic crisis, oil prices fluctuated between $20 and $40 a barrel.

Fluid n.

Meaning

流体

Example

The doctor told his family that he was not allowed eat solid food yet, only fluids.

List 15

Foresight n.

Meaning

深谋远虑

Example

Luckily I’d had the foresight to get in plenty of food.

Forge v.

Meaning

锻造

Example

Our task was now to forge a new brand.

Formulate v.

Meaning

构想出

Example

They intended to formulate a new and simple theory.

Forthcoming adj.

Meaning

即将发生的

Example

Keep an eye on the noticeboards for forthcoming events.

Fortnight n.

Meaning

两星期

Example

Adam can learn a language in a fortnight.

Forum n.

Meaning

讨论会

Example

There is going to be a forum on promotion proposal.

Fossil n.

Meaning

化石

Fragment n.

Meaning

碎片

Example

Esther could hear only fragments of their conversation through the wall.

Freight n.

Meaning

货运

Example

Will the goods be sent by air or sea freight?

Frontier n.

Meaning

边境

Example

Lille is in the north of France and close to the frontier between France and Belgium.

Frost n.

Meaning

霜

Example

A late frost in May could easily kill these delicate plants.

Frown v.

Meaning

皱眉

Example

When the math teacher finished her explanation, she found the students were still frowning.

Fruitful adj.

Meaning

多产的

Example

It was a fruitful discussion, with both sides agreeing to adopt a common policy.

Furnace n.

Meaning

炉子

Example

People who work with furnaces in a steel factory need to wear protective clothing.

Fuss v.

Meaning

（为小事）烦恼

Example

I spent days fussing about the travel arrangements.

Gadget n.

Meaning

小器具

Example

Have you seen this handy little gadget - it’s for separating egg yolks from whites.

Gaol n.

Meaning

监狱

Garment n.

Meaning

（一件）衣服

Example

Many of the garments have the customers’ name tags sewn into the linings.

Gear n.

Meaning

拍档

Example

Helen changed gear as she approached the junction.

Geometry n.

Meaning

几何学

Germ n.

Meaning

幼芽

Example

The germ of a story began to form in his mind.

Glimpse v.

Meaning

看一眼

Example

Susan has been waiting for nearly three hours only for a glimpse at her favorite star.
