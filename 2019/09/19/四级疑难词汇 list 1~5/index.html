四级疑难词汇 list 1~5

发表于 2019-09-19 | 分类于 English-Learning | 评论数： | 阅读次数：

List 1

Abolish v.

Meaning

废除

Example

Till now, some bad customs and laws haven’t been abolished in that small town.

Abundant adj.

Meaning

丰富的

Example

The plaintiff has abundant evidence to prove the stealer’s guilt.

Academy n.

Meaning

研究会

Accord v.

Meaning

使符合

Example

Apple’s products were designed to accord with the boss’s tastes and to meet his extremely high standards.

Accordance n.

Meaning

一致

Example

The rights of children should be protected in accordance with the law.

Accordingly adv.

Meaning

因此

Example

Kelly still considered him as a child and treated him accordingly.

Accountable adj.

Meaning

负有责任的

Example

I have a strong accountable feeling and good interpersonal skills.

Acquaint adj.

Meaning

使认识

Example

Film provides an ideal opportunity to acquaint viewers with something unknown.

Acquaintance n.

Meaning

认识

Acute adj.

Meaning

严重的

Example

The housing shortage is more acute than first thought

Acre n.

Meaning

英亩

Aerial adj.

Meaning

航空的

Example

Meanwhile, the massive aerial bombing of military targets continued unabated

Agenda n.

Meaning

议事日程

Example

The government set an agenda for constitutional reform.

Aisle n.

Meaning

过道

Example

When I go to the cinema, I like to choose the aisle seat.

List 2

Algebra n.

Meaning

代数

Example

Bill was not good at the abstract algebra in high school.

Allege vt.

Meaning

断言

Example

The prosecution alleged that the man had been responsible for the accident.

Alley n.

Meaning

小巷

Example

There are many blind alleys in this town.

Allocate vt.

Meaning

分配

Example

It is difficult for me to allocate my time to swim.

Allowance n.

Meaning

津贴

Example

I have a good retirement allowance that will make it easy for me to buy a nice house.

Ally v.

Meaning

（使）结盟

Example

Italy allied itself with Germany during World War II.

Altitude n.

Meaning

海拔

Example

The altitude was so dizzying that I couldn’t go down the mountain.

Aluminium n.

Meaning

铝

Example

Cover the fish with aluminium foil and cook over a low heat.

Ambassador n.

Meaning

大使

Meaning

Late last night, the French ambassador was summoned to the Foreign Office to discuss the crisis.

Amid prep.

Meaning

在······中间

Example

She may have put her notebook amid the journals.

Ample adj.

Meaning

充分的

Example

I like this apartment because it has an ample living room.

Anchor v.

Meaning

锚

Example

We anchored our ship close to the shore.

Antique n.

Meaning

古董

Example

He is running an antique store, so he can easily find out whether this is real or fake.

Apparatus n.

Meaning

器械

Example

This kind of apparatus can make full use of the oxygen in water.

Applicable adj.

Meaning

能应用的

Example

These signs are applicable only on main roads.

Aptitude n.

Meaning

资质

Example

Anyone who applies for the job will have an aptitude test.

Arbitrary adj.

Meaning

专断的

Example

A good judge does not make arbitrary decisions.

Ashore adv.

Meaning

在岸上

Example

The ship was driven ashore by the bad weather.

Astrophysics n.

Meaning

天体物理学

Example

This module was designed for astrophysics research.

Arithmetic n.

Meaning

算术

List 3

Asset n.

Meaning

资产

Example

Mark’s assets include shares in the company and a house in London.

Attain vt.

Meaning

达到

Example

We give them every encouragement to attain this goal.

Attic n.

Meaning

阁楼

Example

I’ve got boxes of old clothes in the attic

Attorney n.

Meaning

律师

Example

She’s an attorney who advises companies about mergers and takeovers.

Auditorium n.

Meaning

观众席

Example

The auditorium could accommodate at least 300 people.

Authentic adj.

Meaning

真实的

Example

However, the British don’t have a history of exporting their foodstuffs, which makes it difficult for restaurants in Hong Kong to source authentic ingredients.

Authorize vt.

Meaning

授权

Example

The professor authorized her assistant to grade papers.

Auxiliary adj.

Meaning

后备的

Example

The hospital has an auxiliary power system in case of blackout.

Awe n.

Meaning

敬畏

Example

The little boy gazed at his father in awe.

Axis n.

Meaning

轴

Example

The door to the room pivots on an axis.

Badge n.

Meaning

徽章

Example

All employees have to wear name badges.

Bail v.

Meaning

帮助脱离困境

Example

Some charity organizations have offered to bail out the primary school.

Bait n.

Meaning

鱼饵

Example

What bait did you use to persuade the kids to clean their rooms?

Ballot n.

Meaning

选举

Example

Workers held a ballot on Sunday and rejected strike action.

List 4

Bang v.

Meaning

猛击

Example

Carl picks up the broom and bangs on the ceiling.

Banknote n.

Meaning

钞票

Banner n.

Meaning

横幅

Example

The onlookers were shouting, cheering, and waving banners.

Banquet n.

Meaning

宴会

Example

Last night he attended a state banquet at Buckingham Palace.

Barn n.

Meaning

谷仓

Example

The property includes two fine twelfth-century timber barns, once used for storing grain.

Barrel n.

Meaning

桶

Example

Albert pored a big barrel of water into his mouth.

Batch n.

Meaning

一批

Example

The president has a batch of documents to sign every day.

Battalion n.

Meaning

军队

Example

When war broke out, he joined the second battalion of the Grenadier Guards.

Beam vt.

Meaning

面露喜色

Example

He beamed his approval of the new idea.

Beard n.

Meaning

胡须

Example

Except for the beard, he hasn’t
changed in fifty years.

Bearing n.

Meaning

影响

Example

Regular exercise has a direct bearing on your general health.

Belly n.

Meaning

肚子

Example

Lie down on your belly.

Beloved adj.

Meaning

所钟爱的

Example

Frank never recovered from the pain caused by the by the death of his beloved little daughter.

Beverage n.

Meaning

饮料

Example

There are many different kinds of beverages in the dinner party for you to choose.

Blade n.

Meaning

刀刃

Example

The knife blade is pointing at Peter.

Blast n.

Meaning

爆炸

Example

They were trying to blast the entrance to the cave.

Bleed v.

Meaning

流血

Example

The soldier’s wound must be taken care of immediately, or he would bleed to death.

Blend v.

Meaning

混合

Example

If I went to school, I could blend in with everybody else.

Blond adj.

Meaning

金发的

Example

Rose was born with blond hair and blue eyes.

Bloody adj.

Meaning

(用于加强语气)
非常的

Example

What bloody awful weather!

Blossom v.

Meaning

变得成功

Example

A computer is the most patient teacher, which is why many students blossom when they practice math or spell on it.

Blur v.

Meaning

（使）变模糊

Example

And, as tuitions continue to increase, the line between merit and need blurs.

Bold adj.

Meaning

勇敢的

Example

In a bold move to try and cut pollution, the city council has banned private cars.

Bolt v.

Meaning

闩门

Example

Please make sure that the door and windows are bolted before you leave.

Bond v.

Meaning

（使）结合

Example

They would be bonded together in love for ever.

Bonus n.

Meaning

奖金

Example

Every year, usually in December, we receive a bonus.

肠

Bracket n.

Brag v.

Example

Brandy n.

Brass n.

Meaning

Breakdown n.

Example

Briefcase n.

Meaning

Brochure n.

Meaning

Example

Please send me a copy of your company brochure.

Broom n.

Meaning

扫帚

Brutal adj.

Meaning

残暴的

Example

The child was taken away from his mother’s arms by the brutal soldiers.

Buckle v.

Meaning

用搭扣扣紧

Example

The plane will take off soon; please buckle up your seat belts.

Bud n.

Meaning

叶芽

Example

Buds appear on the trees in spring.

Bulk n.

Meaning

Example

The shop bought drinks in bulk.

Bulletin n.

Example

Bumper n.

Bureau n.

局

Example

Bypass v.

Meaning

Example

The odds of a criminal using technical means to bypass a security system are so small that the FBI doesn’t even track those statistics.

Bystander n.

Meaning

旁观者

Cabinet n.

Meaning

Example

Most of the cabinet members were against the new tax treaty.

Calf n.

Meaning

牛犊

Example

Cane n.

Meaning

手杖

Example

He wore a grey suit and leaned heavily on his cane.
