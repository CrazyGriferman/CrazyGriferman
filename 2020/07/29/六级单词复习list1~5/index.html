六级单词复习 list1~5

发表于 2020-07-29 | 更新于 2020-07-30 | 分类于 English-Learning | 评论数： | 阅读次数：

List 1

Abide the judge’s decision

遵守裁判的判决

Abort the mission

中止任务

walk abreast

并排走

Food was in abundance

食物很充足

hand-made accessories

手工饰品

Be acclaimed by

被···称赞

These results are in accord with earlier research.

这些结果与先前的调查结果相吻合

Acid remarks

刻薄的批评

fields adjoined the garden

田地挨着花园

Affirm the value of public service

肯定公众服务的价值

The economic problems afflict Britain.

经济问题困扰着英国

Keep the company afloat

维持企业的经营，使其能够偿债

In the aftermath of the coup

政变的后果

In the aggregate

总共

Agitate against the Vietnam war

煽动反对越南战争

Scream in agony

痛苦地尖叫

‘What ails you?’ he asked.

“什么困扰着你？”他问

Airborne particles

空气粉尘

Cooking is akin to woodwork

烹饪像木工

Align oneself with the liberals

和自由党结盟

List 2

Be allergic to wool

对羊毛过敏

Form an alliance against the government

建立一个联盟来对抗政府

Allot us two weeks to complete the task

分配给我们两周时间去完成这个任务

Allude to a feeling that

暗示一种···的情感

Allure investors to put money in it

吸引投资者投入资金

Keep the plane aloft

使飞机保持在空中飞行

Amend out reports

修改我们的报告

Run out of Ammunition

没弹药了

Amplify the amount of light

增强房间里的光线

Analogue device

模拟设备

In anguish

痛苦

Annex an area

吞并了一个地区

An anonymous letter

一封匿名信

Acute political antenna

敏锐的政治触觉

An antonym of

···的反义词

Be appalled by

被···的行为吓到

Breathing apparatus

呼吸器

Appease international opposition

平息国际反对力量

Appendix n.

附录

An investment appraisal

一次投资估价

Make sb. apprentice to sb.

让某人成为某人的学徒

Be tied to one’s apron strings

被···管得太严 （围裙带）

An apt description of

对···恰当的描述

Arbitrate their argument

裁决他们的争论

Ardent labours

热情劳动的成果

The political arena

官场(竞技场)

Aristocrat n.

贵族

City’s main arteries

城市的主要干线

Articulate the sensation

把这种感觉表达清楚

Ground-based artillery

陆基大炮

The moon continues to ascend up.

月亮持续升高

Ascertain the facts of the case

弄清这个事件的事实

List 3

Indecent assault

不适当的攻击

Assimilate other cultures

同化其他文化

Go astray

迷路

Asylum seeker n.

寻求政治庇护者

An atlas of the world n.

世界地图集

Augment your wealth

让你的财富增值

an auxiliary means of communication

交流的辅助工具

An autonomous region

一个自治区

No avail

毫无效果

Avenge his father’s murder

向杀害他父亲的凶手报仇

Avert his eyes from that beautiful girl

将目光从那个漂亮女孩身上移开

Barge in the office

闯进办公室

The old army barracks

旧军营

After 15 barren years

在15年没有成果之后

Keep back behind barricades

被挡在栅栏之后

be battered on the head v.

头部被反复击打

A bead curtain

珠帘

Beckon me to come nearer

招手示意我靠近一点

Tumor was benign

肿瘤是良性的

Be beset by mosquitoes

被蚊子困扰

List 4

Beware of ··· problems

注意···的问题

The instructions bewildered me

这个教程使我迷惑

Bizarre behaviors

奇怪的行为

Steep the cloth in bleach

把衣服浸泡在漂白剂里

Trading bloc n.

贸易集团

He already knows how to bluff

他早就会糊弄人了

Blunt our enthusiasm for

减弱了对···的热情

Make sb. blush

让某人脸红

That hardly bolsters his suitability

他这么做依然不合适

bolster up 改善

A bouquet of red roses

玫瑰的香味

A severe bout of flu

一次严重的流感(bout意指时间较短)

Do you really want to boycott her wedding?

你真的要拒绝参与她的婚礼吗？

Our kids need braces

我们的孩子需要依靠

At a brisk pace

以一种很快的速度

She constantly broods about her family

她一直在考虑她的家庭

Bruise one’s knee

某人的膝盖擦伤了

A burglar broke into one’s home

一名窃贼闯入了某人的家

May I butt in on your conversation?

我能插句话吗？

butt in 插嘴

A sense of urgency buzzes around the room.

房间里弥漫着一种危机感

A cabinet member

内阁成员

Steep into a canoe

跨进独木舟

List 5

A canvas bag

一个帆布包

A woollen cape

一件羊毛斗篷

Space capsule

太空舱

Carbohydrate can provide abundant energy.

碳水化合物可以提供丰富的能量

Cardinal points of the compass

罗盘上的基点

A carton orange juice

一盒橙汁

Cast a shadow over

给······蒙上了一层阴影

Casualty figure

伤亡统计数字

Cathedral n.

大教堂

Catholic adj.

天主教的

Be hidden in a secret cavity

被藏在一个秘密的洞内

A widely respected chancellor

一位广受尊敬的校长

chapel n.

小教堂

A church choir

教堂唱诗班（也可用作合唱团）

Strike a cord with his audience

触动了听众的心弦

Chorus the praises of the positive suggestions

不约而同地颂扬政府的积极性建议

Give a chronicle of the war

制作了战争年代的编年史

A chunk of bread

一大块面包

Circus n.

马戏团

A sense of civic pride

一种公民的自豪感

Clamp two boards together

把两块木板固定在一起

Clan n.

宗族

Clench his fist

握紧他的拳头

Clergy n.

神职人员

How cliché is that?

多没创意？

Cling to

紧紧抓住

It is difficult to cloak a sin.

掩盖罪恶是很难的。

Arteries clogged up

血管堵塞

Closet information

机密的消息

Clutch a photograph

紧握一张照片

Coalition government

联合政府

Coarse skin

粗糙的皮肤

Cockpit n.

驾驶舱
